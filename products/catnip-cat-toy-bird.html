FAQ ▾

How often should I add fresh catnip?

Most cats respond best when you refresh weekly. If interest fades sooner, add a small pinch and crinkle the wings to release scent.

Is the pocket secure?

Yes — Chirpy’s refill pocket uses a secure closure to hold loose catnip. Always press it fully closed before play.

Will all cats react to catnip?

Not all cats respond — sensitivity is hereditary. If yours isn’t a catnip fan, try silvervine or valerian blends.

How do I clean the toy?

Surface clean with a damp cloth and let air dry. Do not soak or machine wash, as this can reduce scent and affect materials.
