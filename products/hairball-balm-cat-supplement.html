How should I give this product to my cat?

Offer a pea-sized amount once daily, straight from the tin or on your fingertip for your cat to lick. You can also spread it on a Cloud9 Lick Mat or mix into a little wet food if preferred.

Will this stop hairballs immediately?

Every cat is different. Some owners notice their cat seems more comfortable within days, while others see the best results after several weeks of consistent daily use. Regular, small amounts are more effective than occasional larger servings.

Is your product formulated by vets?

Our balm is not directly formulated by veterinarians. However, it is built on well-researched ingredients widely recognised for supporting coat health, digestion, and natural hairball passage. All amounts are kept within safe ranges for daily use.

Can all cats use this product?

Yes, it is suitable for any cat over 6 months old. For kittens under 6 months, please speak with your vet for age-appropriate guidance.

What if my cat has health conditions?

If your cat has an existing medical condition or is under veterinary care, we recommend checking with your vet before introducing any new supplement or balm. This ensures it fits alongside their ongoing treatment.

Can this be used alongside other C9 supplements?

Yes. Hairball Balm can be used alongside other Cloud9 supplements as part of a daily wellness routine. Many owners pair it with our powders for complete coat and digestion support.

Can this be used long-term and every day?

Absolutely. This balm is designed for daily use, and for best results it should be given consistently over time. Long-term use helps support coat condition, digestive comfort, and easier natural hairball passage.
